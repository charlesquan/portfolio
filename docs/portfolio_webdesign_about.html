About

Melle started out as a mascot character I drew for fun but as an animator, I often think about what an animated show featuring my characters would be like as well. The original concept for Melle's show was a series of dialogue-free skits but it developed into vlogs as my own take on a genre that was popular at the time.

The first episode was created with 2D vector graphics but as the series progressed, different techniques were tried out including motion capture, 3D with cel-shading and even being rendered using a game engine.

Even though many different styles have been utilised over the course of the series, Melle's design has pretty much stayed the same since the beginning. Advances in technology may have some graphical influences on the series but the heart of the show will never change.
